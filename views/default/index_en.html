Engineering graduates Follow-Up Information System - UVM Hermosillo

The system was designed with the purpose of supplying Universidad del Valle de Mexico´s Engineering college directorate, in Campus Hermosillo, with useful and relevant information on the graduates. Each student that graduated from the alma mater, is automatically assigned to a user account and a password, subsequently informed of these data through an email containing a link to the system. Graduates answer questions and provide personal, postgraduate and occupational information. The directorate has access to the database and statistics.
